Wood Varnish

“Wood varnish painting is a finishing technique used to protect and enhance the natural beauty of wood surfaces. Varnish is a transparent or semi-transparent coating that is applied to wood to provide a durable, glossy, or matte finish, while also protecting the wood from moisture, scratches, and wear.”
